About PunePropAI

Revolutionizing Pune real estate with AI-powered property valuation and market intelligence

Our Story

PunePropAI was born from a simple observation: the Pune real estate market was becoming increasingly complex, with home buyers and sellers struggling to get accurate, data-driven property valuations.

Founded in 2023 by a team of data scientists and real estate experts, we set out to create a platform that combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with deep local market knowledge to provide the most accurate property valuations in Pune.

Today, we've helped over 5,000+ homeowners make informed decisions about their properties, with our AI models analyzing thousands of data points to deliver valuations with 95% accuracy.

Our Mission

To democratize access to accurate real estate information and empower every Pune citizen to make confident property decisions through transparent, data-driven insights.

Our Vision

To become Pune's most trusted real estate intelligence platform, setting new standards for accuracy, transparency, and user experience in property valuation.

Our Values

Accuracy First

We prioritize precision in every valuation, constantly refining our AI models with the latest market data.

Transparency

We believe in complete transparency about how our valuations are calculated and what factors influence them.

User-Centric

Every feature we build is designed with our users' needs at the forefront, making complex data accessible.
