Hinjewadi

₹7,200/sq.ft

Hinjewadi is Pune's primary IT hub, home to Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park. The area offers excellent opportunities for IT professionals with high rental demand and strong capital appreciation. Ongoing infrastructure development is improving connectivity and amenities.

Key Features

  • Food courts & malls
