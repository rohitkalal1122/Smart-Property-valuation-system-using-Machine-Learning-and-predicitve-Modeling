RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority)

Mandatory for projects above 500 sq.m. Protects homebuyers' interests and ensures project completion.

Stamp Duty & Registration

Currently 5-7% in Maharashtra. Must be paid within 4 months of agreement execution.

GST on Under-Construction

1% for affordable housing, 5% for other residential properties on under-construction units.

Credit-linked subsidy scheme available for first-time home buyers in certain income categories.
